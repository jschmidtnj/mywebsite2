    # getting email working

    After trying to get mailinabox, postfix, and others working on a gcp server, essentially creating my own mail server, I realized that it wouldn't work and looked for alternatives. Thankfully I found this - improvmx, which forwards emails to your favorite email client (gmail). To send emails from your domain, you need to follow these instructions. It's really easy and it just works. That's why I'm sticking with it.
